Information about Francesco and Jane Bienni

Francesco and Jane Bienni have been married for 25 years, both are Italians and were born in Venice, but they are extremely passionate about France and its culture and that's why they got married in the country and lived there for 2 years, after a while they adopted the idea of move to San Francisco and open a French restaurant to satisfy someone who was also passionate or wanted to know a little more about the culture.

Restaurant Information

The restaurant started small but with a lot of love and affection from Francesco and Jane, it soon became popular with the population of San Francisco and got bigger and bigger until they had to move to a place with more space which made it possible to also open a place for special events.

Marriage Report

It was a very special day for me, we had a wonderful ceremony and we took several photos for registration, we also managed to gather all our family and we had a lot of fun, Said Jane
